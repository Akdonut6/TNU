BACKGROUND STORY

Welcome to THE NEXT UPDATE – A game of hopes and dreams where one challenger falls and another claims victory and the NEXT UPDATE.

In THE NEXT UPDATE, challengers from abandoned games gather to fight for a chance to breathe life back into their worlds. Seeking glory for their game and with goals and dreams of their own, they will each do anything to win. In a tournament to decide the best of them all, we will see a clash of characters from completely different worlds seeking triumph against foreign foes.

KEY FEATURES:

  • Engaging Storyline: Immerse yourself in a story where challengers from different worlds come together each holding their own stories and goals.
  • Challenging Gameplay: This game will host input-based combat creating a high skill ceiling with unique and complex mechanics.
  • Multiplayer: THE NEXT UPDATE is a local multiplayer game with a focus on fun competition between friends.

THE CHALLENGERS

MEN IN SUITS

The man in the suit. A replaceable mob character whose only role is to die over and over again. Wanting to break free from the monotonous lifestyle he seeks a way out. Chanced with the opportunity he will do anything to win The Next Update and escape his cycle of replacement.

MOONSHADOW

After being captured and taken to the facility he was treated like an animal. He found a way to escape the desolate inhumane facility and finally gained freedom. He quickly realized he had only stepped out into a wider prison where he would have to hide and cover every trace, only dreaming to create a society where everyone was welcomed. Chanced with the opportunity to make his wish come true he dauntlessly charges into the competition seeking his Next Update.

GRANDPA LARRY

Once a powerful archmage, now an old man forgotten by time. His knowledge of forbidden magic makes him a dangerous opponent despite his age. He enters the tournament to prove that wisdom can overcome youth and to reclaim his lost legacy.

S.ARMSTRONG

A massive brawler with overwhelming physical power. His sheer strength and durability make him a formidable opponent. He fights not for glory, but to test his limits against the strongest challengers the multiverse has to offer.
